How It Works

Our simple, step-by-step process guides you from self-discovery to college application success.

1

Share Your Story

Answer our holistic questionnaire, designed using principles from developmental psychology and narrative therapy to understand you deeply.

You'll also provide your activities, GPA, SAT/ACT scores, and estimated course grades. No detail is too small—from family responsibilities to personal hobbies.

2

Witness Your Cognitive Blueprint Emerge

Our seven-agent AI team analyzes your profile from every angle. They identify your unique intelligences, map your developmental journey, and reframe your experiences into powerful strengths.
